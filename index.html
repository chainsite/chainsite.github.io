The Definitive Cross-Chain service

  • Problem

    ETH gas is extremely high, pricing out retail. However, projects on ETH are much more mature, creative, and robust in design. Since non-whales are priced out of ETH, they can only explore its copycat projects and native innovators on BSC. Since BSC gas is cheap and there are token bridges between ETH and BSC, it’s possible to batch entries and exits of positions ETH projects directly from BSC, by sharing gas.

  • Solution

    This is a cross-chain service on BSC that allows users to enter and exit ETH ecosystem projects (can include other L1 blockchains too) and positions while using BSC as the settlement layer.

  • How?

    We can create a self-balancing liquidity pool between ETH and BSC using the token bridge. Users can choose to enter into positions from BSC, the capital enters the BSC pool. Once enough orders of the same strategy is queued, the ETH pool executes the transaction on behalf of users and accounts for gains/losses. When a user wants to exit, the BSC pool can provide the initial liquidity for exit, and queue a batched removal on the ETH side.

    As long as there’s enough capital between the ETH and BSC pools, they can autobalance themselves periodically for constant deposit/withdrawal liquidity, optimized by machine learning.
